# Sales-Supply-Chain-Executive-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing unit profit, inventory reorder levels, and regional sales distribution.
Dashboard Components & Visuals
Total Profitability (KPI Card): A high-level summary showing $187.3... in total unit profit, giving stakeholders an immediate view of financial performance.
Inventory Management (Column Chart): "Minimum Reorder Quantity by Category." This identifies which product categories (like Beverages and Dried Fruit) require the highest stock levels to maintain operations.
Profit Composition (Pie Chart & TreeMap): * The Pie Chart breaks down the percentage of unit profit across categories.
The TreeMap provides a hierarchical view, making it easy to see that "Dried Fruit & Nuts" and "Beverages" are the dominant profit drivers.
Geographical Sales (Map): Visualizes the "Count of ID by City," showing the density of sales across major North American hubs like Boston, Chicago, and Denver.

Business Insights

Top Performers: Dried Fruit & Nuts and Jams/Preserves contribute significantly to the unit profit margin.

Inventory Strategy: Beverages have the highest minimum reorder quantity, suggesting they move quickly or have longer lead times from suppliers.

Regional Reach: The map indicates a strong presence in the Eastern and Midwestern United States, which can help target future marketing efforts.
